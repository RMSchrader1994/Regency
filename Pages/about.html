Welcome to my website, dedicated to providing quality, Regency-inspired garb for the curvier lady in the United Kingdom. My desire to create these dresses for others arose from my own interest and participation in Regency-themed activities. That is, in addition to being a bit of a ‘Janeite’, I love a good Regency Ball (although I have to admit to lacking any dance skill whatsoever) and attend the Jane Austen Festival in Bath whenever possible. Indeed, as retirement is fast approaching, I hope to attend many more similar events in the future.

It was while attempting to source day-dresses, ball gowns and the odd Spencer to wear while attending such events that I realised there was very little available which was suitable in terms of size, flattering style, fit, and cost. I found plenty for slimmer ladies, but not for those of us who may be a UK size 18 or over. Consequently, I began to make my own, and following numerous enquiries from other like-minded souls, I decided to make my frocks for others who may be experiencing the same sourcing difficulties.

This venture is very much in its infancy, so while there may not be a huge range listed at any one time this is due to the time it takes to produce the standard of finish I am attempting to achieve, together with the fact that I am currently running up my creations while continuing to hold down my ‘day job’ in the NHS. As a rule of thumb, it takes approximately 10-14 days for a new dress to be sewn and listed.

Please do not be disheartened if there is nothing available in your size or within your budget at the time of your visit; I may well be hard at work producing something that will be right up your street – so do visit again soon.
